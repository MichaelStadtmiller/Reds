These are the rules

Each player guesses how many wins the Reds will have at the end of the 2016 regular reason.

Whichever player is closest to the total Reds wins will win the bet. The winning guess may be under or over the actual wins. (not like The Price is Right rules.)

Tie Breaker

Each player also guessed the total Reds stolen bases for the year. Whoever is closest (same rules as above) will win the tiebreaker. If this is a tie too, then, well.. I don't know. Call Gil and he'll flip a coin or something.

Note from the developer

Each bar has different colors. This is what they mean:

  • Green on Left - Number of games won so far.
  • Red on Right - Number of games lost so far.
  • Grey in Middle - Number of games left.
  • Projection % - Number of wins / number of games played.
    • * This is a pretty bad projection. It will move a lot throughout the season to keep it interesting...
  • Green in Middle - Wins projected based on current projection %
  • Yellow in Middle - Your guess
  • Score - Number of games between your guess (yellow bar) and the wins projected (green bar). Low score wins!
