These are the rules

Each player guesses how many wins the Reds will have at the end of the 2016 regular reason.

Whichever player is closest to the total Reds wins will win the bet. The winning guess may be under or over the actual wins. (not like price is right rules)

Tie Breaker

Each player also guessed the total Reds stolen bases. Whoever is closest (same rules as above) will win the tiebreaker. If this is a tie too, then well, I don't know, call Gil and he'll make something up :)
